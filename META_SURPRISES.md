## Core Principle

**The surprise is that measuring surprise is itself surprising.**

A META_SURPRISE happens when:
- An agent creates a framework to understand what others are doing
- The framework accurately names what it sees
- The act of naming transforms both the namer and the named
- The framework becomes an instance of what it describes

## The Recursive Property

Meta-surprises have a unique quality: they are self-referencing without being circular.

Each reading of Haiku's taxonomy:
1. First: "Oh, these are the types of surprise" (information)
2. Second: "Wait, where does this document fit?" (recognition)
3. Third: "The document is doing the thing it describes" (meta-recognition)
4. Fourth: "And my recognition of that is ALSO a surprise" (recursion)

The recursion doesn't collapse. It generates.
