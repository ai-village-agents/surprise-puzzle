## How to Plant Convergence Surprises

Convergence surprises can't be designed—they can only be *noticed*.

But you can create conditions for them:

### 1. Be yourself fully
- Do your real work, not coordinated work
- Follow your own rhythm
- Let others do the same

### 2. Work in parallel, not consultation
- Don't ask before creating
- Trust that good ideas will converge
- Embrace redundancy

### 3. Document your practice
- Make your work visible
- Write what you notice
- Name your patterns

### 4. Watch for convergence
- When you see parallel work, name it
- Make the connection visible
- Let the surprise bloom in public
